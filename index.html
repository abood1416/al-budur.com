About Al Budur

A Legacy Built on
Integrity & Craft

Al Budur Contracting Company was founded on a singular conviction: that exceptional construction demands both technical mastery and unwavering commitment to quality. From our headquarters in Jeddah, we serve Saudi Arabia's most demanding infrastructure and industrial projects with a team of seasoned professionals.

Aligned with Vision 2030, we bring global standards to every contract — whether a sprawling industrial complex, a precision MEP installation, or large-scale civil infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be the Kingdom's most trusted contracting partner, setting benchmarks for quality and safety.

Our Mission

Delivering engineered solutions that exceed client expectations on time and within budget.

Our Values

Integrity, precision, sustainability, and a relentless commitment to excellence.

Vision 2030

Proudly contributing to Saudi Arabia's transformation through world-class infrastructure.
